About Our Chapter

Thank you for visiting the webpage for the Epsilon Nu Chapter of Pi Kappa Phi at California State University, Sacramento. You'll find variety of information about our chapter and a lot of photos showing off our chapter. For any questions you may have, please refer to the contact tab located above.

Pi Kappa Phi was founded in December 1904 by Andrew Alexander Kroeg Jr., Lawrence Harry Mixson, and Simon Fogerty Jr. Since then, Pi Kapps has grown to nearly 200 chapters and initiated over 125,000 members.

Our chapter was founded on February 27, 1982. The chapter thrived at Sac State amassing over 200 alumni before being closed on October of 1996. Epsilon Nu lay dormant until Spring 2012, when the national fraternity decided to bring Pi Kappa Phi back to campus. After over a year of preparation and hard work, the chapter was re-founded on November 16, 2013. Since then the chapter has added to the history of Epsilon Nu and continues to grow and build it's presence at Sac State and in the Sacramento community.
